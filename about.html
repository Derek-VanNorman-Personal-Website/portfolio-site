When I am not attending Fullstack Bootcamp, being a father to my two kids, or a husband to my lovely wife I am doing one of the following.


Building

Last year my wife and I took a chance and sold our home in town to aquire 10 acres in the country. Our end goal is to raise our children on a homestead of sorts. This property will eventually have gardens, a pond, food plots for hunting and other activities for us and our kids to participate in.


Biking

My family and I spend a great deal of time outside. Our kids are still very young so one of the activities we enjoy doing is biking. My wife and I have a toddler/child seat attached to each of our bikes so our children can ride along. This activity is great for getting us out of the house as a family while working on our fitness


Hunting

When I get time during the fall I try to spend as much time in the woods as possible. I enjoy sitting in nature and watching the wildlife. Of cousrse the goal is to procure food to eat, but most of the time I just watch. I also am a conservationist that enjoys making the land better suited for local wildlife to thrive.
